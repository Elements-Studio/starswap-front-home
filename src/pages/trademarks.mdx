# Acceptable Uses

You may do the following _without_ receiving specific permission from Starswap Labs:

- Use Starswap wordmarks in text to truthfully refer to and/or link to unmodified Starswap smart contracts, protocols, interfaces, programs, products, services and technologies (&quot;Starswap software&quot;).

- Use Starswap wordmarks to clearly signal to users that there is no affiliation with or endorsement by Starswap Labs.
- Follow the terms of the open source licenses for Starswap software.
